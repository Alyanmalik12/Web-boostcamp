Welcome to Visualizease

"An Asset Discovery and Network Visualization Tool For Industrial Control Systems"

Maximize Visibility. Minimize Surprises.

At Visualizease, we offer visibility for all your endpoints through extensive data collection. This enables us to uncover vulnerabilities and pinpoint areas where your risk management efforts should be focused. Our objective is to assist you in:

  • Visualizing device connections and traffic patterns to support research and compliance endeavors.
  • Forseeing security threats before they disrupt your operations, while mitigating risks throughout your organization.

Asset discovery in OT and IoT environments can be completely passive, achieved by observing mirrored traffic without interfering with critical processes, triggering alarms, or generating additional traffic. We help you obtain a comprehensive overview of communicating devices and traffic patterns, creating a visualization map that accelerates research and swiftly identifies anomalies and incidents.
